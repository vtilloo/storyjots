Eddie Red Undercover by Marcia Wells

★★★★

Age Rating: 8-12 Years

Meet Eddie Red, a sixth-grader with a photographic memory and incredible drawing skills. When the NYPD discovers his talents, they recruit him as their secret weapon to help catch art thieves. Working undercover with his best friend and a detective, Eddie must use his unique abilities to solve the case before the criminals strike again.
